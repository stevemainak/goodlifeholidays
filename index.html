Welcome to GoodLifeHolidays

It's time to start your adventure

Discover the magic of Kenya — from the golden savannahs of the Maasai Mara to the sun-kissed beaches of Diani. Whether you're chasing wildlife, culture, or serenity, we're here to make it unforgettable.

At GoodLifeHolidays, we don’t just plan trips — we craft experiences. Our tailored tours take you beyond the guidebooks into the heart of Kenya, where each journey tells a story, and every destination leaves a mark.

Search Destination

Activities

From thrilling safaris in the wild plains to hot air balloon rides over the Rift Valley, Kenya is packed with unforgettable adventures. Dive into the turquoise waters of the coast, hike the scenic trails of Mount Kenya, or explore vibrant local cultures. There's something for every kind of explorer.

Travel Arrangements

We handle the logistics so you can focus on the experience. From airport pickups and drop-offs to booking accommodations, flights, and transfers, our team ensures smooth, stress-free travel from start to finish — whether you're a solo traveler or part of a group.

Private Guide

Enjoy the insights and expertise of our local guides who bring Kenya's rich heritage to life. With a private guide, you’ll get in-depth knowledge of the landscapes, cultures, and wildlife while enjoying personalized attention throughout your journey.

Location Manager

Our on-ground location managers ensure your trip runs like clockwork. They coordinate with service providers, monitor itineraries, and are your go-to support team for anything you need — so you're always in safe hands.
