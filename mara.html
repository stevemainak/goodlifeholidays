3 DAYS Masai Mara Migration Private Safari

#Day 1 - Nairobi to Masai Mara

Wake up as early as 0630am, then proceed to our dining hall where you will find your Buffet Breakfast served by our cook.
You will have 30min of breakfast and there after leave for the whole day game drive packed with your picnic lunch.
You will start spotting animals at a very close distance and we advise our guests to have all their cameras ready as the animals are in their Natural habitats and actions takes place without a notice lunch will be served under a tree enjoy fresh air as you unwind. You will be able to see the hippo pool where with luck you may spot crocodiles basking on the rocks.
Numerous plains game including antelopes, wildebeest, zebra, Masai giraffe can be seen as well as scavengers like hyena and vulture. There after lunch you will proceed for another afternoon game drive as you return to the camp at around 1700hrs. Overnight and Dinner at the Camp.
